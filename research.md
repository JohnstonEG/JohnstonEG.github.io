## Working Papers

**Displacement and Task Reallocation: Evidence from the PSID**
with Ali Yousuf (Illinois State University)
*Draft available upon request*

This paper examines how displaced workers reallocate across task types following involuntary job loss. Using plant closures as a natural experiment in the Panel Study of Income Dynamics matched with O*NET task measures, we estimate the causal effect of displacement on task composition using a difference-in-differences framework with Callaway-Sant'Anna, propensity score matching, and inverse probability weighting.

**Sports Betting Legalization Spillover Effects in International Betting Markets**
*Presented at the Midwest Economics Association, 2025*

This paper investigates whether the repeal of PASPA generated spillover effects in international sports betting markets. Using a difference-in-differences design on 73,652 bookmaker-sport-league-month observations across three European sportsbooks from 2013 to 2024, I decompose bookmaker margins into three components and estimate the effect of U.S. legalization on closing odds abroad.
